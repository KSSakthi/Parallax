Pandas are known for their distinctive black and white fur patterns.

Pandas primarily eat bamboo, which makes up around 99% of their diet.

🐼🐼

Pandas have a thumb-like extension on their paws that helps them grasp bamboo stalks.

Pandas are solitary creatures and often prefer to be alone.

🐼🐼🐼

Pandas are native to China and are considered a national treasure.

Pandas have a special diet, but their digestive system is more similar to that of a carnivore.

🐼🐼🐼🐼

Despite their appearance, pandas belong to the bear family and are skilled climbers.

Pandas communicate with each other through vocalizations and scent marking.

🐼🐼🐼🐼🐼

Pandas have a low reproductive rate and are at risk due to habitat loss.

Pandas are often used as symbols of conservation efforts and environmental awareness.
